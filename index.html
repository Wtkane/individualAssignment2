Visit Italy

A guide to the most beautiful cities on earth

Why Choose Italy?

I recently traveled to Italy in the winter of 2023 and wanted to share my experiences with others who were thinking about visiting! Italy is a beautiful country and I hope everyone gets the chance to visit at some point during their life. This website documents my experiences in the three distinct Italian cities of Rome, Florence, and Venice. These cities are unique and brimming with culture. I hope you are able to learn about Italian culture and hope it inspires you to plan your visit!

Different Cities

With 391 bridges over waterways and no cars allowed, Venice is an extremely picturesque city. Visitors should explore the Doge Palace and other architectural marvels.


This is Florence, a city that is widely viewed as the birthplace of the Renascence. Its architecture and food are unparalleled. Visitors are even able to take a day trip to Tuscany for wine tastings


This is A View Of Rome. It is known as a vast city filled with gardens, art, and ruins that tell of the glory of the Roman Empire. Rome contains many popular attractions like the Colosseum and the Vatican.
